About Frenchloveria

Welcome to Frenchloveria — Jaipur's most trusted destination for mastering the French language. Founded with a singular passion for linguistic excellence, we have been transforming students into confident French speakers for over a decade.

At Frenchloveria, we believe that language is more than words — it is culture, connection, and confidence. Our carefully crafted curriculum spans all DELF levels (A1 to C2) and is tailored to meet the requirements of CBSE, ICSE, RBSE, and International boards, catering to students from Grade 4 all the way to Grade 12.

We combine the richness of French culture with modern teaching methodologies — making learning not just effective, but genuinely delightful. Whether you dream of studying in Paris, cracking board exams, or simply speaking the language of love fluently, Frenchloveria is your launchpad.

Nos Cours

Levels We Teach

From absolute beginner to advanced mastery — we guide every student through the official DELF/DALF framework with personalised attention.

A1

Débutant

Beginner

Start your French journey from scratch. Learn greetings, basic vocabulary, simple sentences and everyday expressions.

  • Basic phrases & introductions
  • Numbers, colours, family
  • Simple present tense
  • Listening & reading basics
CBSE · ICSE · RBSE · Intl · Gr 4–12
A2

Élémentaire

Elementary

Build on the basics. Communicate in simple, routine tasks and exchange information on familiar topics.

  • Past & future tenses
  • Daily routines & travel
  • Shopping & services
  • Short descriptive writing
CBSE · ICSE · RBSE · Intl · Gr 4–12
B1

Intermédiaire

Intermediate

Gain independence in the language. Express opinions, narrate events and handle most situations while travelling.

  • Expressing opinions & feelings
  • Complex grammatical structures
  • Extended reading & writing
  • Cultural immersion activities
CBSE · ICSE · RBSE · Intl · Gr 6–12
B2

Avancé

Upper Intermediate

Achieve near-fluency. Understand complex texts and interact with native speakers without preparation.

  • Advanced grammar & syntax
  • Essay & report writing
  • Debate & argumentation
  • Literature appreciation
CBSE · ICSE · RBSE · Intl · Gr 8–12
C1

Autonome

Advanced

Express yourself fluently and spontaneously. Use the language for academic, professional and social purposes.

  • Academic & professional French
  • Complex argumentation
  • Advanced listening comprehension
  • Research & presentation skills
International · Gr 10–12
C2

Maîtrise

Mastery

Master French at the highest level — equivalent to an educated native speaker. Perfect for university and diplomacy.

  • Native-level fluency
  • Literary & academic analysis
  • Simultaneous comprehension
  • Diplomatic & formal French
International · Gr 11–12
